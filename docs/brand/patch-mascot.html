The strawberry poison dart frog is the size of a thumbnail, but predators learn to leave her alone fast. The colour is a warning. Visible from across the forest floor — long before you get close enough to regret it.

That's the gig for agentic‑security. Small footprint. Loud signal. Doesn't bite first. But you'd better read the warning before you ship.

Patch lives at the threshold between what your AI just wrote and what reaches your users. She is calm, specific, and impossible to ignore.

01 Construction. geometric / circle-based / single weight

One body, five circles.

Patch is built entirely from ellipses and circles on a single grid. The two eye bumps share the body's silhouette — no outline, no line weight. Geometry does the work.

BODY
Ellipse, 164 × 112. Centered.
BUMPS
Two r=36 circles, ±36 from center, on the top quadrant.
CORNEAS
Two r=24 circles, concentric with bumps.
PUPILS
r=14, nudged 2px right & 3px down for life.
SHINE
r=5 highlight, top-left of each pupil. Optional secondary r=2.
MOUTH
Single quadratic arc, 2.6 stroke. Never closed.
FRECKLES
6–8 random circles, r 2.5–4, on the back hemisphere only.
FEET
Toe-pad clusters: ellipse + three r=5 circles. Three toes only.

DON'T · DISTORT
